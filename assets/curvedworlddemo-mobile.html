--- permalink: demo/curvedworlddemo-mobile.html --- Enter fullscreen and rotate your device in landscape mode for the best experience.

Controls

• Use the toolbar to select your (fluid) input mode, size and amount.
• Place fluid/terrain by touching the planet.
• Rotate/Zoom the planet using the onscreen joysticks.
• Fluid Sources can be removed by tapping on them.
• Switch level using the bottom left dropdown.
• Performance varies per device.
