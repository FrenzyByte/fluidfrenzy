What is Fluid Frenzy?

Welcome to Fluid Frenzy! Fluid Frenzy is an interactive GPU-accelerated fluid simulation and rendering tool designed for use with Unity Terrain and Custom terrains. This powerful asset provides developers with a wide range of tools to create realistic, immersive, and dynamic fluid simulations in their Unity projects. Whether you want to simulate water, lava, erosion effects, or terraforming, Fluid Frenzy has got you covered. With its fluid simulation components, rendering capabilities, and fluid modifiers, you can create stunning visual effects and dynamic interactions in your projects. Explore the documentation to learn more about the key features, setup instructions, sample scenes, and the various components and modifiers available in Fluid Frenzy. Get ready to elevate your Unity projects with realistic and engaging fluid simulations! Let the waves of creativity flow with Fluid Frenzy!.
